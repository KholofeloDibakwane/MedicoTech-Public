About MedicoTech™

Expert-driven medico-legal and psychological reporting services powered by innovation

Who We Are

We provide expert-driven medico-legal and psychological reporting services for healthcare providers, legal firms, and insurance companies. Our services are powered by proprietary systems —PsycheWriter™ and PsychometryWriter™— developed to enhance efficiency and consistency without compromising quality or professional judgment.

We combine clinical expertise with technology-enabled innovation to set a new standard in mental health and medico-legal reporting—helping organisations eliminate bottlenecks, improve reporting accuracy, and restore focus on patient care.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading partner for expert-driven medical reporting solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver efficient, accurate, consistent, and customisable reports that empower healthcare and legal systems to meet growing demand while upholding professional excellence.

About The Founder

Meet the visionary behind MedicoTech™—a clinical psychologist and health-tech innovator committed to transforming psychological reporting through expertise, innovation, and integrity.

Thato Masia

Founder & Director, MedicoTech™

Thato Masia is a Clinical Psychologist and health-tech innovator passionate about transforming the way psychological and medico-legal reporting is delivered. With a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology from Rhodes University and a reputation as a trusted mental health voice on national media platforms, Thato brings both expertise and vision to the field.

Recognised as an Alan Gray Fellow for her entrepreneurial leadership, Thato founded MedicoTech™ to address one of the industry's most pressing challenges: delays and inefficiencies in psychological reporting. Her approach blends deep clinical knowledge with technology-enabled processes, ensuring that organisations receive accurate, consistent, and timely reports without compromising professional standards.

"Mental health systems can't afford to be slowed by administrative bottlenecks. At MedicoTech™, our mission is simple: to give organisations timely, consistent, and expert-driven reports—so clinicians can focus on care and patients can access justice without delay."

— Thato Masia, Founder & Director, MedicoTech™

Thato's work is amplified by her collaboration and research with South Africa's leading healthcare and academic authorities such as Prof. Solly Rataemane, a renowned psychiatrist and former Head of the Department of Psychiatry at Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University; Dr. Itumeleng Funani, a health governance strategist with deep expertise in partnerships across South Africa's Department of Health and innovation ecosystems; and Dr. Jacob Poo, a specialist in digital health systems and clinical integration.

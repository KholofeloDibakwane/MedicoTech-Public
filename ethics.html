Our Ethical Framework

Guided by principles that ensure responsible reporting and patient welfare

At MedicoTech™, we recognise that ethical considerations are as critical as clinical accuracy in psychological reporting. Our framework is built on foundational pillars that ensure our services enhance, rather than replace, the essential human element in healthcare while maintaining strict compliance with all regulatory requirements.

We maintain rigorous quality control processes to ensure every report meets the highest professional standards.

Data Privacy Protocol

Our security-first approach to confidential information

Security by Design

We implement multiple layers of protection including temporary working memory, automatic redaction tools, pseudonymization techniques, and end-to-end encryption. The clinician maintains full control over what information enters our systems.

  • Closed Systems: All data processed within secure, proprietary environments.
  • Limited Retention: Client information is retained only as long as necessary.
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access to sensitive data.
  • Audit Trails: Comprehensive logging of all data access and modifications.

Ethics FAQ

Addressing common questions about our compliance standards

All reports undergo rigorous review by our team of clinical psychologists before delivery. Our systems provide structural support but never autonomous conclusions - the clinician remains the sole author of all diagnostic interpretations.

We are fully compliant with HPCSA guidelines for psychological practice and POPIA regulations for data protection. Our systems undergo regular security audits and all team members complete annual ethics training.

We implement military-grade encryption for all data transmissions, pseudonymisation techniques during processing, and strict access controls. Full reports are never permanently stored in our systems.

We maintain clear version control and audit trails for all reports. Amendments are processed through the same rigorous review pipeline as original reports, with clear documentation of all changes.
